RETURNING CUSTOMER DEFINITION

A returning customer is someone who has:

  • Previously submitted at least 1 receipt.
  • Completed OTP verification before.
  • Has their data in our system.
  • Can be identified by their phone number or email.

ENTRY METHODS

  1. PRIMARY: Scan QR Code at a participating shop.
    • Automatically identifies the shop location.
    • URL: campaign.richs.com.my/?shop_id=XXX
  2. SECONDARY: Direct URL access.
    • Manual shop selection is required by the customer.
    • URL: campaign.richs.com.my

TECHNICAL NOTES

Session Management:

  • Returning customer session: 30 minutes active.
  • After timeout: Requires fresh OTP verification.

Data Pre-Population:

  • Name, IC, Email are pulled from the database.
  • Shop location is from last use or QR code scan.

Security Measures:

  • Device fingerprinting and IP tracking are active.
  • Behavioral analysis for fraud detection.

KEY NOTES FOR MARKETING

  • Streamlined Experience: The key benefit is minimizing friction for loyal customers with no OTP and pre-filled forms to encourage repeat participation.
  • Identification Method: Phone or email is used for one-click verification, making re-entry simple.
  • Security Balance: The flow is convenient but remains secure with measures like session timeouts and backend fraud detection.
  • Data & Engagement: The dashboard and stats provide a personalized experience, showing customers their progress and increasing engagement.
